3D Projects

Mountain Range Project

The Mountain Range project was made solely using Autodesk Maya 2014 and Adobe Photoshop. The scene includes use of numerous Mental Ray materials, effects such as fog and the Maya Ocean shader. The mountain range is developed using a nurbs plane along with the ripple effect generated with the ocean shader. After tweaking with render settings, eliminating snow and introducnig fog, the final render was obtained using Mental Ray.

Z Stool Concept

The Z Stool Concept is a concept design of a Z shaped concrete and wood based stool. The central frame consists of wood and the main material used alongside the framework is concrete. The model was developed using Autodesk Maya 2015 and implements bump mapping to give a more realistic concrete finish. The scene was rendered using Mental Ray.
